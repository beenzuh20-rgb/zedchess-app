Community Guidelines

Last updated: {{ now.year if now else "2026" }}

Be Respectful

Treat other players with respect in chat, messages, and challenges. Harassment, hate speech, discrimination, and threats are not tolerated.

No Cheating

Engine assistance, shared accounts, move/clock manipulation, and collusion undermine fair play and are prohibited. Violations lead to forfeit and bans.

Play Fair, Bet Responsibly

Betting is for entertainment. Only stake what you can afford to lose, and never pressure others to wager. Underage betting is forbidden.

Appropriate Content

Do not post spam, scams, sexually explicit, violent, or illegal content. Avatars and bios should remain appropriate for all ages.

Reporting

Use block/mute to manage unwanted contact, and report serious abuse to the administrators. False reports may themselves be actioned.

Consequences

Breaches may result in a warning, mute, suspension, balance adjustment, or a permanent ban at the discretion of the operators.
