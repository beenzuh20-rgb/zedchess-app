Privacy Policy

Last updated: {{ now.year if now else "2026" }}

1. Information We Collect

Account data you provide (username, email, password hash), profile data (avatar, bio, country), and gameplay data (moves, results, ratings, stakes). We also store technical logs such as IP address and last-seen time for security and anti-abuse.

2. How We Use Information

To operate matches, maintain ratings and leaderboards, settle bets, prevent cheating, send notifications, and improve the Service. Email is used for account recovery and important notices.

3. Wallet & Financial Data

Balances and transactions are stored to provide deposits, withdrawals, and payouts. We do not store raw payment credentials; payments are handled by a payment provider when integrated.

4. Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may disclose information to comply with law, enforce our Terms, or protect users and the Service. Public profile data (username, rating, avatar) is visible to other players.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain data while your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and personal data, subject to legal and transactional record-keeping obligations.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed, sessions are protected with CSRF tokens, and traffic should be served over HTTPS in production. No system is perfectly secure; use a strong password.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may access, correct, or delete your personal data, and object to certain processing. Contact support to exercise these rights.
